Dog shelters house too many dogs and they all need care in the form of food, medical facilities, and a safe and protective shelter. In order to establish such shelters, they need help from the community or the government. Across the world, millions of dogs get euthanized simply because there is no room anymore and they have not been adopted. If the shelters had more resources, this can be avoided. However, it is not only money that can help. People can contribute with their time, energy, donate food, and bedding, etc.
